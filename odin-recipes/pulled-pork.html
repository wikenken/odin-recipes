North Carolina Pulled Pork

Description

Easy to made Pork from North Carolina. Very easy to make which makes this foolproof for beginners

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds pork
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste
  • 1/2 cup ketchup
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup red wine vinegar

Directions

  1. Preheat slow cooker on Low for 15 minutes
  2. Season pork shoulder with salt and pepper; place pork in preheated slow cooker
  3. Mix ketchup, brown sugar, and vinegar in a bowl; pour over pork
  4. Cook on Low for 8 hours
  5. Transfer pork to a large platter and slice into 3 to 4 pieces
  6. Shred meat with 2 forks and return to slow cooker
  7. Continue to cook for 1 hour
  8. Ready to serve!
